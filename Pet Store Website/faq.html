Product Information

What products do you offer at Hairy Paws Online Pet Store?

We offer a wide range of products for your furry friends, including pet food, pet toys, and pet accessories.

Do you carry specific brands of pet food?

Yes, we offer a variety of high-quality pet food brands to cater to different dietary needs and preferences.

Can I find specialty or prescription pet food at your store?

Yes, we stock specialty and prescription pet food for pets with specific dietary requirements. Please contact our team for more information.

What types of pet toys do you sell?

We have a diverse selection of pet toys, including interactive toys, chew toys, and puzzle toys to keep your pets entertained and mentally stimulated.

Are your pet accessories limited to specific animals?

No, we have accessories for a wide range of pets, including dogs, cats, and small animals like rabbits and hamsters.

Online Shopping and Services

Can I purchase products in-store, or is it only online?

You can shop both online and at our physical store. Visit our store location for an in-person shopping experience.

Do you offer home delivery for online purchases?

Yes, we offer home delivery services for online orders. Check our website for delivery details and options.

What payment methods do you accept for online and in-store purchases?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and cash for in-store purchases. Online payments can be made through secure channels.

Can I track my online order once it's been shipped?

Yes, you will receive tracking information for your online orders. You can use this information to monitor the status and delivery of your package.

Pet Grooming Services

Do you offer pet grooming services at Hairy Paws Online Pet Store?

Yes, we provide pet grooming services. You can schedule a grooming appointment through our website or by calling our store.

What types of grooming services do you offer?

We offer various grooming services including spa, haircuts, and undercoats. Check our website or contact us for a complete list of services.

Do you have professional groomers on staff?

Absolutely! Our experienced and trained groomers are dedicated to keeping your pets looking and feeling their best.

Can I book a grooming appointment online?

Yes, you can conveniently book grooming appointments through our website or by giving us a call.

What safety measures are in place at your physical store to protect pets and customers?

We prioritize the safety and well-being of pets and customers. Our store follows strict hygiene and safety protocols, including regular cleaning and sanitization.

Yes, we provide customer parking for your convenience when visiting our physical store.
